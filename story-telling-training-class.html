Story Telling Training Class

About the Class

The Storytelling Training Class helps children enhance their speaking skills, creativity, and self-confidence.

Through engaging stories, expressive delivery techniques, and group activities, kids learn how to tell a story vividly and clearly — building both language ability and presentation skills.

Age: 7 – 12 Years Old

Time: 1:00 pm – 7:00 pm

Details:

  • ✔ How to structure a story (beginning, development, climax, conclusion)
  • ✔ Use of tone, facial expression, and body language to enhance storytelling
  • ✔ Strengthen verbal communication in Chinese or English
  • ✔ Build stage confidence and presentation skills
