About Us

Hao Xue Yue Centre's Theme Song

Our Background

Hao Xue Yue, A Second Home for your child. Based in Mahkota Cheras, Selangor, Malaysia, is a before and after school care and tuition center dedicated to primary school children.

We provide a space where children can learn, grow, and feel safe — giving parents peace of mind while at work.

Centre's Education Concept

The Meaning Behind Hao Xue Yue

(Hao) represents a loving after-school care program where children grow in independence and develop strong character.

(Xue) represents our homework and tuition classes, fostering a serious attitude toward studies and providing dedicated tuition courses for children.

(Yue) represents being immersed in the charm of classical music, inspiring artistry and patience while growing happily.

These three characters together not only convey our center’s core services and philosophy but also reflect our hopes for the children.

Here, children develop good character and learning habits, growing happily in a loving environment.

Hao Xue Yue, is also a homophone for “good academics.” It signifies that children, with good character and a strong learning attitude, combined with joyful growth through music, will achieve excellent academic performance and well-rounded development.
