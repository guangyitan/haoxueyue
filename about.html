About Us

Our Background

Hao Xue Yue, based in Cheras, Malaysia, is an afterschool and tuition center dedicated to primary school children.

We provide a space where children can learn, grow, and feel safe — giving parents peace of mind while at work.

Our Mission

We believe that learning should be enjoyable and growth should be joyful.

Our mission is to support children's development holistically — academically, emotionally, and creatively — through structured guidance and enrichment.

Educational Philosophy

We uphold:

✔ Small group classes that respect each child’s learning pace

✔ Good habits in study and time management

✔ A love for learning and confident self-expression

✔ A nurturing environment that combines care, discipline, and responsibility
