Hao Xue Yue Centre's Theme Song

Our Background

Hao Xue Yue, based in Cheras， Selangor, Malaysia, is a before and after school care and tuition center dedicated to primary school children.

We provide a space where children can learn, grow, and feel safe — giving parents peace of mind while at work.

Centre's Education Concept

The Meaning Behind “Hao Xue Yue”

The name “Hao Xue Yue” carries thoughtful meaning:

• Hao (好) represents care and our day care services

• Xue (学) stands for academic support and tuition

• Yue (乐) symbolizes music, enrichment, and joyful learning

Together, “Hao Xue Yue” reflects our mission to nurture children with good character, a strong learning attitude, and joy in their educational journey.
